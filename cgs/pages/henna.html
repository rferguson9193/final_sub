HENNA

CGS Henna and Art specializes in creating intricate henna designs perfect for all occasions! Henna can be worn to weddings, festivals, and any occasions! These beautiful patterns last around 2 weeks  and are so fun to apply!      

Designs can be personalized to a client's liking! If you need help picking a design, Siji can help with that too, she can also go freehand!   The options are limitless! 

Henna prices but the base price is 10 dollars.  Contact Siji Sabeer if you are interested.  Contact information is at the bottom of the page.
