ART CLASSES

Digital-Class 

Join our art teacher, Siji Sabeer for an hour each week! Siji teaches students of all ages via Zoom. They work with all mediums and learn something new each week! Check out Siji's instagram to see some of her student's works.

Self-Paced 

Take your time to create what you would like. Once a week, you can meet Siji and create a drawing of your choice with her guidance! You will walk out with a new art piece feeling very proud of yourself!

In-person

Once a week, come hang out with Siji and learn how to draw something new each week! Every class leads you through a project step-by-step and you walk out with a beautiful creation!
