SIJI SABEER

Artist and Henna Specialist

Siji Sabeer is an Artist and Henna Specialist based in Harrisburg, North Carolina. She specializes in various forms of custom art, designing, henna, an as an art teacher who has taught over 200 students of all ages. She is also a professional makup artist and does makeup for various events. Siji was born in Kerala, India and was raised in Qatar where she did her schooling in Doha. She movd back to India to get her Bachelors in Technology. She has had a passion for art since she was kid and started doing henna at the age of 10. During her college years, she did henna and art as side job while working in engineering college for 3 years. After her wedding, Siji moved to Charlotte and while job hunting decided to pursue her passion for art as a full time profession. She has been in the art industry since 2015. Siji has won several awards in henna and visual arts. She has also designed logos for many companies and even got a chance to work a a title coordinator for a Bollywood series. Since 2020, she has been working with a world renowned artist to develop her knowledge in art.  If you are interested in knowing more about what Siji can offer please check her website and social media networks.
