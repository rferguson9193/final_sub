CUSTOM ART 

CALLIGRAPHY 

Calligraphy is the art of forming beautiful symbols by hand and arranging them well.  CGS Henna and Art specializes in custom calligraphy. If you are interested in purchasing a calligraphy piece, please contact Siji Sabeer.

MURALS  

Murals are paintings applied to and made integral with the surface of a wall or ceiling. Murals can easily elevate any room and add in a splash of color. If you are interested in adding a mural to your wall, please contact Siji Sabeer.       

CARICATURES

Caricatures are made by exaggerating personality and behavior, as well as physical appearance. They are created for a humorous effect and bring fun to anyone. If  you are interested in a personal caricature, contact Siji Sabeer.      

CGS Henna and Art provides affordable custom art pieces. We cover a range of products and various canvas sizes.

Custom pieces are personalized to a client's taste and are not limited to the products mentoned above.  

Custom art prices vary  by canvas size, but if you are interested  in requesting  a  custom art piece, contact Siji Sabeer.  Contact information is at the bottom of the page.
